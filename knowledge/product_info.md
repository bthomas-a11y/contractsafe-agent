# ContractSafe Product Knowledge Base

## What Is ContractSafe?
ContractSafe is contract lifecycle management (CLM) software designed for teams who want power without the pain. It covers intake to execution to renewal with zero bloat and no steep learning curve.

## Key Differentiators
- **Unlimited users** on all plans (no per-seat pricing)
- **Fast setup:** Most teams live in under 30 minutes
- **Transparent, affordable pricing** (not quote-based enterprise pricing)
- **Ease of use:** Consistently top-rated for ease of use on review sites
- **Real human support** included in every plan (dedicated customer success manager)
- **No IT department needed** to implement

## Core Features
- **AI Contract Analysis:** Automatically extracts key terms, effective dates, expiration dates, renewal deadlines, party names, key clauses
- **AI-Powered Search:** Plain-English search ("contracts expiring in 2 weeks," "vendor agreements over $50k")
- **OCR Technology:** Makes scanned documents fully searchable
- **Contract Repository:** Centralized, searchable storage
- **Alerts & Reminders:** Proactive notifications for renewals, milestones, deadlines
- **eSign Integrations:** Built-in electronic signature workflow
- **Custom Dashboards & Reports:** Without needing spreadsheet expertise
- **Version Control & Audit Trails:** Full history tracking
- **Enterprise-Grade Security:** SOC 2, GDPR, full audit trails
- **Approval Workflows:** Route contracts for review and sign-off
- **Contract Templates:** Standardized starting points for common agreements

## AI Philosophy
- "Co-pilot, not autopilot" -- AI assists humans, doesn't replace them
- Human-in-the-loop design: AI suggests, humans approve
- Focused on practical, immediate value vs. futuristic promises
- Solves today's problems: data extraction, search, organization
