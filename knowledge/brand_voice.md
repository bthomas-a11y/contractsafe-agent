# ContractSafe Brand Voice Guide

## Core Voice Characteristics
- **Conversational:** Write like you're explaining to a colleague over coffee
- **Meandering:** Take the scenic route. Digressions are features, not bugs.
- **Metaphor-rich:** Use extended metaphors as structural scaffolding
- **Self-aware:** Meta-commentary, asides, parenthetical humor
- **Story-driven:** Complete narratives with setup, context, buildup, payoff
- **Trust the reader:** They'll follow tangents if you bring them back

## Voice DON'Ts
- No corporate/B2B jargon ("leverage," "streamline," "drive efficiency")
- No punchy fragment-sentence hooks
- No mode switching between casual explanation and formal writing
- No "In conclusion," "Furthermore," "Additionally," "Moreover"
- No summarized stories (always full narrative arcs)
- No rushing to the point

## Voice DOs
- Philosophical connections ("Everything has a lifecycle...")
- Self-corrections and asides ("(cycle?)")
- Direct address ("Here's the thing though")
- Tangential digressions that connect back
- Specific, invented scenarios over abstract claims
- Extended metaphors that structure entire sections
